Project

Blast Magazine was a short-lived magazine that aimed to chronicle the Vorticist art movement of the 1910s. More information on the context can be found at Wikipedia.

This is an attempt to digitise both editions, not just as a PDF or series of scans, but as a responsive and accessible HTML website.

Some / all of the contained material has opinions that are not representative of my opinions. While the project reproduces this as a historical artefact, that is the limit of the intent.
